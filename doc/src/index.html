Introduction

CAMVOX is an open source CAM (Computer-aided manufacturing) application designed for producing multi-axis toolpaths.

  • CSG (Constructive Solid Geometry) [wikipedia]
  • High resolution voxel space [wikipedia]
  • Full 3D milling with 4 or 5-axis.
  • Collision avoidance.
